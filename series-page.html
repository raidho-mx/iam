We Are Internet is the first video series that explores the human side of age media.

Across 7 episodes, We Are Internet connects stories and visions of influential people behind leading niche publications, agencies, companies and institutions from around the world. They share the how's and the why’s of emerging mindsets, lifestyles and perspectives, flying under the radar of mainstream media.

Our Prupose

We are publishing these video series to start a conversation about understanding internet as culture, from the context of media and creativity. We think it is time to reflect about these fast-paced, early days of the 21st century, to be able to build better futures, together.

About This Episode:

“Any way you can describe Humanity, that’s how you can describe the internet”, says Heidi Hackemer (Founder & Director of Strategy in Wolf & Wilhelmine). Find out how the internet culture made us more equal than ever, and has enabled the human to be no longer voiceless; and our generation to leave it’s mark.
